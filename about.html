Our Story

SMARTPACK was created to solve the frustration and stress caused by forgetting important items every day.

Why We Started

Students and office workers constantly forget important items like homework, chargers, keys, laptops, and notebooks. Existing reminder apps only remind people manually and do not physically check if the items are packed.

SMARTPACK combines AI technology with smart inventory scanning to make organization easier.
